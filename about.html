Our Story

Helping kids discover the joy of creativity beyond screens

Our Mission

At Chatterpillar Club, we believe in the power of imagination to transform childhood. In a world increasingly dominated by screens, we create magical coloring experiences that help children explore their creativity while developing fine motor skills and focus.

Our founder, Emily Carter, started Chatterpillar Club after noticing how her own children were spending more time with devices than with crayons. What began as handmade coloring books for her kids has grown into a movement to bring back hands-on creative play.

"Every child deserves the chance to create their own magical worlds, not just consume someone else's."

- Emily Carter, Founder

Our Values

Creativity First

We design our products to spark imagination and original thinking rather than passive consumption.

Child-Centered

Every product is tested with real kids to ensure it's engaging, age-appropriate and fun.

Eco-Conscious

We use sustainable materials and encourage reuse through digital downloads.
